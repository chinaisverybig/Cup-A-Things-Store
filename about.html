About

Cup A’ Things was founded by two university students who were always short on time yet, wanted a savory and delicious meal. As university students, we spent most of our time at school, working part-time jobs and spending time with our friends and ... family, which meant we had little time to cook a meal. Cup A’ Things was created in hopes that we can provide students with cheap, time efficient and most importantly, delicious meals. The exclusive factors that make us so different and exceptional from our competitors is that we are compassionate, empathetic and offer your cravings at the lowest price guaranteed.

WHO WE ARE

MISSION STATEMENT

Cup A’ Things is a major international instant noodle importer and exporter, which headquarters in Canada. Our company sells all brands of Asian instant noodles. Our mission statement is to provide our customers with an increase in convenience and accessibility towards low priced instant noodles. The target market is set at a global scale, which includes anyone from anywhere. A small sector that we put an emphasis on is, post-secondary students that are within a budget and are short on time. Our company aims to expand our product availability beyond the Asian suppliers within the next two years.
