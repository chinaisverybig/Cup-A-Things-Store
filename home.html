Cup A' Things

Cup A’ Things is a major instant noodle importer and exporter, which headquarters in Canada. The company sells all brands of Asian instant noodles worldwide. The purpose of the company is to create convenience for its consumers, as instant noodles is convenient, and not having to go to the store to purchase it is an additional convenience. The potential customers would vary across a wide range of people, but a large portion of the market would be young post-secondary students with little to no money leftover to spend on an extravagant meal. Instant noodles can be eaten in many occasions from eating at home by yourself while binge watching movies to having a party with your friends who all crave some instant noodles. Our company stands out among other instant noodle importers, because we have every brand and flavour of instant noodle in our hands, and we sell at the lowest prices possible.
