LAKSA

DESCRIPTION

Prima Taste’s Laksa was established in Singapore to satisfy the creamy and sour cravings of fellow Singaporeans. Since then this instant noodle has gained the popularity worldwide and has been a staple for many families and individuals that are in search of a quick meal.

Known for the creamy coconut taste along with the offsetting sour soup base, this Laksa has been a favorite for those that crave traditional laksa noodles. The silky and tender noodles are a great compliment to the jam-packed flavored soup base.
