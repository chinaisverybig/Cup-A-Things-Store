CHU QIAN YI DING

DESCRIPTION

Mi Goreng is the perfect combination of sweet, spicy and savory, this famous Indonesian instant noodle is the perfect balance for those that crave a stir fry noodle. Unlike the typical instant noodle, Mi Goreng does not require a soup base and served best with when stirred with the provided soy sauce, oil, and the seasoning.

Mi Goreng has become one of the most popular stir fry noodle in the world because of its intense flavors and low cost. Derived and inspired from Indonesian traditional fried noodle, Indomie’s Mi Goreng has been the Asia’s most consumed stir fry instant noodle.
